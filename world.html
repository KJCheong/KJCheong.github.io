Around the World

On this page I will be going through differentcars from around the world

Since there are 195 countries on earth.

I will be generaliing them into different region like

America, Europe and Asia.

America

American cars are usually known for their,powerful high horsepower cars that can go from 0-100 in no time at all.

Americans are also famous for their black smoke shooting trucks that are modded with high end parts making them menaces on the road.

Europe

Europe is home to the manufacturers of the worlds fastest cars ranging from high end super cars all the way to high end luxury sedans

Not only are they fast but they also have some of the best mechanics that F1 has ever seen

Asia

Asian cars have been widely known for their ability to be tuned towhatever the driver wants, be it a daily driver, drift car or even a pure horse power machine.

In South East Asia cars here usually specialise in economical fuel efficient daily cars.

Cars in other parts of asia usually are on the edge of technology delivering an innovative driving experience for all.
