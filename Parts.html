What are turbos

There are 6 different types of turbocharger which is commonly known within the automotive industry

Single-Turbo/Twin-Turbo/Twin-Scroll Turbo/Variable Geometry Turbo/Variable Twin Scroll Turbo/Electric Turbo but for the purpose of this I will be refering to single turbos

What are Turbos for

The main purpose of a turbocharger is to increase the volumetric efficiency of the combustion chamber. The compressor of the turbochargers is found using air from the atmosphere which is responsible for increasing the density through the rotation of impeller blade passages.

The turbine of the turbocharger is responsible for producing high back pressure on the exhaust manifold which results in the higher exhaust gas pressure as compared to the atmospheric pressure.

What is suspension

A cars suspension system is a protective lattice of shock-absorbing components such as springs and dampers.

It helps ensure that your drive is safe and smooth by absorbing the energy from various road bumps and other kinetic impacts.

Suspension

Suspensions comes in all shapes and sizes with some being better than others and some being purpose built for special vehicles.

Changing the suspension of cars has been a staple for car enthusiasts and the like with some suspensions even allowing the car to bounce off the road.

What is an exhaust

The exhaust system not only makes your car sound good but getting a better can actually help in terms on performance, with the main purpose being to filter out harmful gasses from the engine to the outside

some trade off you get are that some exhausts systems may not have a in built catalytic converter leading to more fumes being released by the car

Different Exhausts

There are many different kinds of exhaust with single exit exhaust being the most common in most commercial cars with some opting for dual exit in sports cars.

whilst changing he exhaust may yield varyying results, the overall purpose of it stays pretty much the same.

What are body kits

Body kits are a facelift for your car, it takes a conventionally boring looking car and turns it into a work of art.

The idea that cars can be works of art has been able to launch a few groups to mainstream success in the automotive industry with brands like Rocket Bunny, Liberty Walk and Brabus just to name a few.

Pros and Cons

All body kits are not built equal, similar to normal products its quality is usually reflected by how much you pay for them.

Some pros of getting a good body kit is not only does it look better, the cars aerodynamics can also be effected in a way that can benefit the overall performance of the car.

The opposite could be said about bad body kits with some being so bad they turn your car into a moving pile of scrap that is not only a danger to other but the dirver too.
